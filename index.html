Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cushion and pillow covers

Should the cushion cover be bigger than the cushion? ‚ñº
Always choose a cushion pad the same size or a few centimeters bigger than your cushion cover to ensure extra plumpness and comfort. Never go for a cushion pad smaller than your cover, as you'll end up with empty corners where there's no filling ‚Äì overstuffed looks and feels much better.
What is the normal size of a cushion cover? ‚ñº
Most cushions come in a square shape. The commonly available sizes are 12"x12" (30cm x 30cm), 16"x16" (40cm x 40cm), 18"x18" (45cm x 45cm), and 24"x24" (60cm x 60cm). However, we offer custom sizing for any dimension you need.
How do I measure for a cushion cover? ‚ñº
For removable covers: 1) Remove the pillow cover from the insert/filler. 2) Turn the cover inside out and lay it on a flat surface. 3) Measure from the inside seam (length + width) with a flexible tape. Use our online measuring tool for accurate custom sizing.
What material is best for cushion covers? ‚ñº
Our polyester and Olefin blend fabrics are ideal for cushion covers. They're durable, easy to wash and maintain, waterproof, UV-resistant, and available at competitive prices. Choose from Cushion Rite, Cushion Plus, Cushion Pro, or Pillow Plus depending on your needs.
How do I clean cushion covers? ‚ñº
Most of our cushion covers can be cleaned in cold water either by hand or on a delicate cycle in the washing machine. Use dye-free laundry detergent for most stains. For outdoor covers, you can also spot-clean with mild soap and water. Always check the care label for specific instructions.
What is your warranty policy? ‚ñº
We offer an industry-leading 7-year warranty on all our cushion and pillow covers. This covers manufacturing defects, fabric deterioration, and zipper failures. We stand behind the quality of our products and are committed to your satisfaction.
